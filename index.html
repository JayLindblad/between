How It Works

A book is left somewhere — a park bench, a coffee shop, a train seat. Inside the cover is a small sticker with an address.

Every book has a page here. Its journey is the record of every person who found it, where they were, and what they wanted to say.

To add your chapter

Enter the ISBN from the back of the book above. You'll find the book's page — read where it's been, then add your own entry: where you found it, a photo if you'd like, and whatever you feel like leaving behind.
